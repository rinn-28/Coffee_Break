About Us

Why you should choose us?

Coffee Break was born from our love for coffee. We select the finest beans, craft them with care, and serve them to create an experience that goes beyond just drinking coffee.

Our journey began with a simple idea: coffee should be more than just a drink—it should be a moment of connection. Whether you’re starting your day, meeting a friend, or taking a quiet pause, we believe every cup has the power to bring people together.

At Coffee Break, sustainability and quality go hand in hand. We partner with local farmers to source ethically grown beans, ensuring that every sip supports communities and preserves the environment. It’s our way of making sure your coffee tastes good and does good.

Our Coffee Menu

We believe coffee is more than a drink, it’s an experience. Explore our signature creations and find the flavor that speaks to you.

- Espresso -

A full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in "shots."

- Americano -

Simple yet timeless espresso infused with hot water, delivering a clean, crisp taste with a lighter body.

- Cappucino -

Smooth yet inviting, espresso blended with steamed milk and crowned with airy foam, delivering a balanced flavor wrapped in warmth.

- Latte -

Gentle and comforting, espresso blended with silky steamed milk for a mellow, soothing flavor that warms the soul.

- Flat White -

A warm embrace of bold espresso and velvety microfoam, crafted for those who seek comfort in every sip.

- Mocha -

Rich and indulgent, espresso blended with chocolate and silky milk, offering a sweet harmony that delights every sip.

- Affogato -

Classic espresso poured over vanilla ice cream, combining warmth and sweetness in a delightful treat.

- Machito -

A powerful shot of espresso 'stained' with a dollop of velvety milk foam. Bold, sweet, and perfectly balanced, offering an intense yet elegant coffee experience.
